# Updating a Firewall

A production AI service often requires regular maintenance to support model upgrades, new training data, and other improvements.
Once you've deployed an AI Firewall alongside your production model, you can modify it to reflect these changes, to configure how tests
are run, or to update the alerts and notifications settings.
In this <a class="reference external" href="https://colab.research.google.com/drive/1k3gdNbZ3wAeOd4srvnMP54JErX5oRvpF" target="_blank">Notebook Walkthrough</a>,
we show how to make such changes.
