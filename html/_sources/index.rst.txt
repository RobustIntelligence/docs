Welcome to RIME!
================================

RIME enables comprehensive testing of data and model performance in the crucial pre-development and post-development stages of the life cycle.

To learn more about RIME, head over to `Product Overview <rime/product-overview.html>`__.
For a hands-on introduction to installing and running RIME, check out `Get Started <get_started/installation.html>`__.
For step-by-step guides to key tasks, check out the **Walkthroughs** below.
