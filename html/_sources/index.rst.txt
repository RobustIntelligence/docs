Welcome to Robust Intelligence!
================================

Robust Intelligence enables comprehensive testing of data and
model performance at the pre-development and post-development stages of the model life
cycle. `Looking for the latest version of this documentation? <https://docs.rime.dev/en/latest/>`_.

If you're new to Robust Intelligence or looking for a quickstart guide, refer to **Documentation Home**.
